— Approach

What makes me different

01

Most designers stop at the wireframe. I don't.

Discovery to delivery. Design decisions that move the product, not just the pixels. I stay in the room with engineering until the thing actually ships, because a great design that breaks in development never existed.

02

I question why before I design what.

Beautiful isn't enough. It has to be defensible. Every screen I ship has a business reason behind it, not just a user one, because what works in Figma and what works in the real world are two different products. I close that gap.

03

I think in systems, not screens.

Flows, states, edge cases, handoffs. I design for how people actually move through a product, not how they're supposed to. Friction is invisible until someone trips over it. My job is to find it before they do.
